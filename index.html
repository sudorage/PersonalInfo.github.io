Why?

There has always been a shortage of clients in minecraft that actually care about the users feedback and what they really want. This is why I designed, developed and created this whole client.

This client not only directly profits off suggestions the users leave, but also tries to implement them as well as it possibly can, making this a perfect opportunity for you to design a client of your choice.

Development process

Developing this client has and will be always fun for me. I have learned a ton about java and the minecraft api, aswell as how to properly interact with my users. This project has been going really well so far, making this one of my most successful projects I have ever had my hands upon.

Thanks to everyone leaving suggestions and contributing the client.
